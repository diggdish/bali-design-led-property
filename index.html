Design Process

From first conversation to a clear architectural direction.

The process begins with listening, then moves through site context, concept design, spatial development, and the atmosphere of the finished experience.

  1. 01Discover

    Clarify lifestyle goals, intended use, timing, location preferences, and comfort level.

  2. 02Brief

    Translate aspirations, daily routines, spatial needs, and site context into a focused design brief.

  3. 03Design

    Shape concept design, spatial brief, architectural direction, guest experience, and positioning.

  4. 04Develop

    Refine layout, material language, climate response, and practical coordination details.

  5. 05Experience

    Consider how the architecture feels through arrival, rest, gathering, privacy, light, and landscape.

Why Bali

Architecture shaped by island life.

  • A well-connected island setting with strong global appeal and established hospitality infrastructure.
  • Climate, wellness, food, culture, and community that support long-stay living.
  • Hospitality depth that can inform guest experience and rental-readiness planning.
  • Design-led homes and villas can feel calm, distinctive, and deeply connected to place.
  • Responsible projects benefit from clear briefing, local knowledge, and practical coordination.
